PROVISIONAL ADMISSION INTO JS1 FOR THE 2022/2023 ACADEMIC SESSION

IMPORTANT NOTICE FOR SUCCESSFUL CANDIDATES

1. The cut-off mark is 50%

2. Candidates are to pay a non-refundable acceptance fee of N7,500.00 at the school.

3. Successful candidates are to proceed to the Principal with the following:

i. The N7,500.00 acceptance fee;

ii. A copy of the application form; and

iii. Original and photocopy of hospital birth certificate

4. The admission letter would be issued by the Principal, after checking 3(i), (ii) and (iii) above.

5. Candidates who scored 50% and above are to come for their admission letters as follows:

Date: Wednesday 20th July to Friday 19th of July 2022

Time: (a) 10am - 3.00pm (Monday-Thursday)

(b) 10am - 12.30pm (Friday)

6. The deadline for the payment f the school fees and other applicable fees shall be MONDAY,22nd of July, 2022.

Please note that late payment would not be accepted and would lead to withdrawl of admission.

CONGRATULATIONS TO ALL SUCCESSFUL CANDIDATES AND THEIR PARNETS
